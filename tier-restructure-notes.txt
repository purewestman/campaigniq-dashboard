# Tier Restructuring Notes

## Business Metric Goals per Tier
| Tier         | Bookings USD (non-renewal) | Unique Customers (non-renewal) | Partner-Delivered Services |
|--------------|---------------------------|-------------------------------|---------------------------|
| Authorized   | N/A                       | 1                             | N/A                       |
| Preferred    | N/A                       | 2                             | N/A                       |
| Elite Zone B | $500K                     | 3                             | 5                         |
| Ambassador   | $10M                      | 10                            | 15                        |

## Key Design Decisions
1. Each partner gets tagged to one of 4 tiers: Authorized, Preferred, Elite (Zone B), Ambassador
2. Compliance = Business Metrics MET + Enablement & Certifications MET
3. We have Enablement data already; Business Metrics need manual entry
4. Need a Business Metrics entry form per partner (Bookings USD, Unique Customers, PDS)
5. The system should auto-calculate if a partner meets their tier's business metric thresholds
6. Enablement requirements scale by tier (currently we only have Elite Zone B: 5 SP, 3 TSP, 2 Boot, 1 Impl)
7. Need to define enablement requirements for all 4 tiers

## Enablement Requirements by Tier (from FY27 Compliance Architecture doc)
- Authorized: 1 Sales Pro, 1 Tech Pro (2 total)
- Preferred: 2 Sales Pro, 2 Tech Pro, 1 Bootcamp (5 total)
- Elite Zone B: 5 Sales Pro, 3 Tech Pro, 2 Bootcamp, 1 Impl Specialist (11 total)
- Ambassador: 8 Sales Pro, 5 Tech Pro, 3 Bootcamp, 2 Impl Specialist (18 total)

## Partner Tier Assignment
Based on the SA Elite Partner Gap Analysis doc, most partners are tagged as Elite Zone B.
But we should allow admin to change tier assignment per partner.

## Implementation Plan
1. Add tier definitions with both business metric and enablement thresholds
2. Add businessMetrics field to Partner type (bookingsUSD, uniqueCustomers, partnerDeliveredServices)
3. Add BusinessMetricsContext for manual entry with localStorage persistence
4. Compute overall compliance = enablement compliance + business metric compliance
5. Update all pages to show both compliance dimensions
